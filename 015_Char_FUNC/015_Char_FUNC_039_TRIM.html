字符函数

删除前后空格或其它字符 TRIM

Oracle 


SQL> SELECT TRIM('   Ora   ') A,
  2         TRIM(LEADING '*' FROM '***Ora***') B,
  3         TRIM(TRAILING '*' FROM '***Ora***') C,
  4         TRIM(BOTH '*' FROM '***Ora***') D
  5  FROM
  6    dual;

A      B            C            D
------ ------------ ------------ ------
Ora    Ora***       ***Ora       Ora
