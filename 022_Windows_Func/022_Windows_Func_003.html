下面是用于计算移动平均的例子
ROWS BETWEEN 1 PRECEDING AND 1 FOLLOWING
意味着 物理行从 当前行-1行开始，到当前行+1行结束。

SELECT
  student_name,
  SUM(grade) AS AllGrade,
  SUM(SUM(grade))
    OVER(ORDER BY student_name
      ROWS BETWEEN 1 PRECEDING AND 1 FOLLOWING) AS "3人移动合计",
  AVG(SUM(grade))
    OVER(ORDER BY student_name
      ROWS BETWEEN 1 PRECEDING AND 1 FOLLOWING) AS "3人移动平均"
FROM
  test_course
GROUP BY
  student_name;
