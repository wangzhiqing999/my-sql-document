二进制转十进制BIN_TO_NUM (Oracle Only)

Oracle 


SQL> SELECT 
  2    BIN_TO_NUM(0,0,0,0,1) AS A,
  3    BIN_TO_NUM(0,0,0,1,0) AS B,
  4    BIN_TO_NUM(0,0,1,0,0) AS C,
  5    BIN_TO_NUM(0,1,0,0,0) AS D,
  6    BIN_TO_NUM(1,0,0,0,0) AS E,
  7    BIN_TO_NUM(1,0,1,0,1) AS F
  8  FROM DUAL; 

         A          B          C          D          E          F
---------- ---------- ---------- ---------- ---------- ----------
         1          2          4          8         16         21

此方法对于 某些枚举类型的计算，计算起来很方便。
参数的数量是可变的
